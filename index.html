AGENDA

So.... What will be doing for 2 and 1/2 Days?

  • Learn the Basics!

    We'll teach you Guys the process of website making. It's really not that hard.

  • How Do You Publish websites?

    We'll make 'em legit using github-pages web hosting and convert them into .tk domains, just like this very website

  • Design Objects to Be 3-D Printed

    Using tinkerCAD and SketchUp, and the makerbot 3D printer in the library, we'll design objects to be printed. We'll teach that for those who need help. It's fairly intuitive though :)

  • Watch the magic

    We will chill, bump tunes, and watch the magic happen with the 3D printers in the library.

  • Virtual Reality

    We'll Play with Google Cardboard and discuss how it's possible. What makes it cool? How does it work? What are the applications?

  • Lego Mindstorms

    We'll make robots, using lego mindstorms. We can make remote controlled cars, rubiks cube solver, walking humanoid, whatever you may have seen online or comes to your imagination
